TRAINING


The Tuesday before the regular conference sessions start, there will be a full day of training courses on PostgreSQL and related topics provided by some of the most popular training companies in the PostgreSQL community. Each half-day session is offered at a cost of €150, and each full-day session at €300. This includes: Approximately 3.5 hours of instructor-led training for half day sessions, 7 hours for full-day sessions Lunch A hotel venue Course material, if required All training sessions are held on the Tuesday before the conference. This means that you can either attend one of the full day sessions, or one or two of the half day sessions. It is not permitted to switch to a different session once your registration has been confirmed, unless you contact the conference organizers and get confirmation first. Each training course is provided by a third-party which is wholly responsible for the course content. The proceeds of the training are shared between the company providing each course and PostgreSQL Europe. The following full-day courses are available: PostgreSQL Indexing by Hans-Jürgen Schönig, Cybertec The following half-day courses are available before lunch: PostgreSQL & PostGIS by Gianni Ciolli and Richard Poole, 2ndQuadrant PostgreSQL Performance Tuning by Bruce Momjian, EnterpriseDB And the following half-day courses are available after lunch: Data Processing Inside PostgreSQL by Bruce Momjian, EnterpriseDB Writing & using Postgres Extensions by Dimitri Fontaine, 2ndQuadrant Note! Therese are overlapping trainings. You can only attend either a full day course, or one half day course before lunch and/or one after. They are all on the same day, and you cannot freely move between the sessions. If you wish to take a training course, you must register in advance when you register for the conference. Places are limited, so book early to avoid disappointment!
